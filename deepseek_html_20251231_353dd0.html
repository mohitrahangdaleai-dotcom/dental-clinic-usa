About Our Practice

Meet Dr. Jesse Massie, DDS

Dr. Jesse Massie grew up right here in Cheyenne, Wyoming, and is proud to serve his community as a trusted family dentist. With over 15 years of experience, Dr. Massie is dedicated to providing gentle, high-quality dental care for patients of all ages.

At Cheyenne Family Dental Center, we believe in treating our patients like family. Our friendly staff, comfortable office, and commitment to outstanding service make us the preferred dental practice in Cheyenne.
